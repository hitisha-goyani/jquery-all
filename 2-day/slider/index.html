Rose

The rose is a type of flowering shrub. Its name comes from the Latin word Rosa.

Sunflower

The common sunflower is a species of large annual forb of the daisy family Asteraceae.

Spring Marguerite

They are part of the wider daisy family and look very similar to Chrysanthemums.

lotus

Lotus flowers appear most prominently in wetlands and usually grow in mud.

Lily

Lilies are usually raised from bulbs, but they can be grown from seed.

Tulip

ulips are spring-blooming perennial herbaceous bulbiferous geophytes in the Tulipa genus.

Poppy

The flowers of most poppy species are attractive and are widely cultivated as annual or perennial.

Gerbera

Gerbera species are tufted, caulescent, perennial herbs, often with woolly crown, up to 80 cm high.
